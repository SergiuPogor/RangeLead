## 🧠 What is RangeLead?

> **[RangeLead.com](https://rangelead.com)** is a **B2B lead intelligence platform** that gives sales teams, agencies, and closers instant access to verified US business contact data — filtered by the problems those businesses actually have.

Instead of buying generic contact lists that waste your time, RangeLead lets you find businesses that **already need what you sell** — before you ever make first contact.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary><b>Is there a subscription or recurring fee?</b></summary>
<br/>
No. RangeLead operates on a pure one-time purchase model. You browse, filter, preview the list, buy it once, and download your CSV. There are no monthly fees, no annual plans, and no lock-in of any kind.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How accurate is the contact data?</b></summary>
<br/>
All contacts go through validation before being added to RangeLead — email syntax and MX verification, phone number formatting and carrier checks, and address geocoding. Lists are refreshed regularly to remove stale entries. No database is perfect, but RangeLead is significantly cleaner than what you'll find from bulk list brokers.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What exactly are "problem indicators"?</b></summary>
<br/>
Problem indicators are data signals attached to each business that reveal a specific operational gap or pain point — things like "no website," "under 3 Google reviews," "no active social profiles," or "recently opened." When you filter by a problem indicator, you're only downloading businesses that already need the solution you sell. It's pre-qualification built into the data.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I use the CSV in my existing tools?</b></summary>
<br/>
Yes. The CSV is clean and standard-formatted, ready to import into any CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, GoHighLevel, etc.), cold email tool (Instantly, Lemlist, Apollo, etc.), or phone dialer. No special import templates needed.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Do you cover all US states?</b></summary>
<br/>
Yes. RangeLead covers businesses across all 50 US states. You can filter by individual state, metro area, or region depending on your campaign needs.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How is RangeLead different from Apollo, ZoomInfo, or Hunter?</b></summary>
<br/>
Those platforms are built for enterprise B2B (finding contacts at large companies). RangeLead is purpose-built for US small and medium business outreach — the kind of businesses that agencies, consultants, and local service providers sell to every day. And critically, RangeLead's problem indicators give you pre-qualified leads that no other platform provides.
</details>
